Want a copy of SMITE?Hey folks!

You’ve probably heard us talk about, or have seen us play a whole lot of Smite. It’s a free-to-play game on PC, and is coming to the Xbox One later this year.

Well… we have a TON, and I mean a TON of early access keys for Smite on Xbox One!
All you have to do is comment on this journal, and you’ll receive a private message (on the RoosterTeeth site) with your code.

If you play Smite on the PC, don’t worry- you’ll be able to merge your account with the Xbox One version and copy over progress and items.

We’ll be streaming some Smite later today, so keep an eye out. Don’t forget to leave a comment to get your key!

Have fun! You’ll have a SMITE-y good time.

First up, congrats to...

JDunlap!

And second, congrats to...

Twobit_!

If you're curious how the winners were selected, all 600+ entries were read, then a top 50 we liked were chosen, and then random selection for the top winners. There's no way to justify one entry being better than the other, as everyone is a fan for their own reasons - just know how much we appreciate every single one of you, and how amazing it was to read your stories and experiences.
